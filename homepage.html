About Us

Welcome to ecotone - sustainable lifestyle! This is a website to help you be more sustainable in your daily habits. This website will cover ways to be more sustainable from your bathroom, beauty and household products, to clothing, and eating habits. We will give you tips and tricks on where to find and how to choose alternatives, without compromising quality. We will advise on different brands, from different price ranges so that you can find what suits you best. We will keep you up to date with environmental issues worldwide and teach you what to pay attention to when making choices.
